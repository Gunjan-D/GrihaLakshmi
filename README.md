## The Problem No One Talks About

| Fact | Source |
|------|--------|
| India has **250 million homemakers** | Census 2011 |
| Their combined unpaid work is worth **₹22.7 lakh crore/year** — larger than the entire IT industry | ILO, 2022 |
| **81% of Indian women** do daily unpaid domestic work. Only 26% of men do. | NSS 2019 |
| Indian women spend **7.5 hours/day** on unpaid work. Men spend 2.5 hours. | Time Use Survey 2019 |
| Only **19% of Indian women** have independent access to a bank account | NFHS-5 |
| In 2021, Madras HC valued a homemaker's contribution at **₹3 lakh/month** | Court record |

**Not a single matrimonial platform in India acknowledges this.**

---

## What GrihaLakshmi Does

GrihaLakshmi is an interactive value estimator that calculates — in rupees — the real market worth of a homemaker's daily contributions, using actual 2024 rates for professional equivalents.

**Users input:**
- Family size
- Work situation (full-time homemaker vs. working + homemaker)
- Daily duties across 7 categories (cooking, childcare, cleaning, laundry, errands, elder care, household management)

**They receive:**
- A personalised **monthly salary estimate** (₹18,000–₹55,000+)
- Annual and career-total valuations
- A **shareable social caption** for Instagram/WhatsApp
- A **pledge flow** — committing a monthly amount to her personal account
