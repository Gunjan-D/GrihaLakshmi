## The Problem No One Talks About

Not a single matrimonial platform in India acknowledges what a homemaker is worth. And now, the institutions that run this country are saying it out loud.

| Authority | What They Said | Year |
|---|---|---|
| **Reserve Bank of India** | Homemaker work = 26–36% of India's GDP | 2024 |
| **Supreme Court of India** | *"How can a homemaker's income be less than a daily wager?"* | Feb 2024 |
| **12 State Governments** | 118 million women now receive cash transfers for household work | 2023–24 |

> The RBI is not a feminist NGO. The Supreme Court is not an activist organisation. The policy has already started. GrihaLakshmi is ahead of the curve, not behind it.

---

## What GrihaLakshmi Does

An interactive value estimator that calculates in rupees the real market worth of a homemaker's daily contributions, using actual 2024 rates for professional equivalents.

**Input**

| What the user provides |
|---|
| Her name and family size |
| Work situation — full-time homemaker or working + homemaker |
| Daily duties across 7 categories: cooking, childcare, cleaning, laundry, errands, elder care, household management |

**Output**

| What they receive |
|---|
| A personalised monthly salary estimate (₹18,000–₹55,000+) |
| Annual and 25-year career-total valuations |
| A shareable social caption for Instagram / WhatsApp |
| A pledge flow — committing a monthly amount to her personal account |
